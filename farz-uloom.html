Essential knowledge for every Muslim's daily life.

Course Overview

The Farz Uloom Course is designed to provide every Muslim with the essential Islamic knowledge required in daily life. It covers the fundamentals of Iman, Salah, Wudhu, Ghusl, and basic rulings of Fiqh. Is course ka maqsad har Musalman ko un ke deeni faraiz se agah karna hai taake wo apni Ibadat sahi tariqe se ada kar sakein.

Who is this course for?

Adults & Youth

Perfect for those who want to fulfill their religious obligations correctly.

Beginners

Anyone looking for a clear path to understanding basic Fiqh.

Teacher Roadmap (Step-by-Step)

Step 1: Faith & Aqaid

Correcting beliefs about the Oneness of Allah and Prophethood.

Step 2: Purity (Taharat)

Learning the practical method of Wudhu and Ghusl.

Step 3: Prayer (Salah)

Perfecting the movements and recitations in daily prayers.

Step 4: Social Manners

Islamic ethics (Akhlaqiyat) and handling daily life interactions.

Learning Outcomes

  • In-depth knowledge of basic Fiqh rules.
  • Correct method of all five daily prayers.
  • Understanding of Halal and Haram in daily life.
  • Memorization of essential masnoon Duas.
