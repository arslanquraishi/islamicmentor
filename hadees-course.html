Course Overview

The Quran-O-Hadees Course is designed for students who want to deeply understand the message of the Quran along with authentic sayings (Ahadith) of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ. Ye course un logon ke liye hai jo sirf tilawat hi nahi balkay Quran ke mafhoom aur Rasool Allah ﷺ ki sunnat ko apni zindagi mein shamil karna chahte hain.

Who is this course for?

Spiritual Seekers

Jo log apne Iman ko mazboot karna chahte hain.

Students of Deen

Jo Tafseer aur Hadith ki gehri samajh hasil karna chahte hain.

Families

Jo apne gharon mein Islami mahol paida karna chahte hain.

Teacher Roadmap (How we Teach)

Step 1: Textual Recitation

Teacher pehle Quranic ayaat ki sahi tilawat (Tajweed) par focus karte hain.

Step 2: Word-to-Word Translation

Ayaat ka lafzi tarjuma samjhaya jata hai taake vocabulary behtar ho.

Step 3: Context & Hadith Linkage

Ayaat ka pas-e-manzar (Context) aur unse judi Ahadith-e-Mubaraka parhai jati hain.

Step 4: Practical Application

Teacher batate hain ke in sabaq ko rozana ki zindagi mein kaise amal mein laya jaye.

What You Will Learn?

  • Tafseer of selected Surahs with authentic context.
  • Study of 40 important Ahadith for daily life.
  • Difference between Sahih, Da'if and Hasan Hadith.
  • Practical Sunnahs of eating, sleeping, and manners.
