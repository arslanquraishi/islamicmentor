Course Overview

The Arabic Grammar Course is designed to give students a strong foundation in classical Arabic. This course helps in understanding the language of the Quran and Hadith directly without translation. Quran-e-Pak ka asli maza aur uski gehrai tabhi samajh aati hai jab aap Arabic Grammar (Sarf-o-Nahw) par uboor hasil karte hain.

Who is this course for?

Quran Students

Those who want to understand the Quranic verses without relying on translations.

Aspiring Scholars

Students who want to pursue higher Islamic studies (Dars-e-Nizami basics).

Our Teaching Roadmap

Step 1: Sarf (Morphology)

Learning how Arabic words are formed from 3-letter roots and their different scales.

Step 2: Nahw (Syntax)

Understanding the rules of combining words to make meaningful sentences.

Step 3: Vocabulary Building

Focusing on the most common words used in the Quran and Ahadith.

Step 4: Live Translation

Applying grammar rules to translate Quranic verses in real-time during class.

What You Will Learn?

  • Foundations of Nahw and Sarf.
  • Ability to recognize Arabic root words.
  • Sentence construction and basic Arabic conversation.
  • Understanding of 70-80% of Quranic vocabulary.
