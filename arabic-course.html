Arabic Grammar Course

The Arabic Grammar Course is designed to give students a strong foundation in classical Arabic. This course helps in understanding the language of the Quran and Hadith directly without translation. From basic rules of Nahw (syntax) and Sarf (morphology) to sentence construction and comprehension, the course builds your ability to read and understand Arabic texts with clarity and confidence.
