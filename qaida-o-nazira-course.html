Course Overview

Learn Quran Online with Quran Nazra (Quran reading) and become a reciter of Quran with proper pronunciation and correct origins. Being a Muslim, it is our religious duty to learn to read Quran correctly as per its commandments.

In this course, you will learn the basics of Arabic letters, their sounds, and how to connect them to read verses fluently. If you have any doubts that your Quran recitation is not up to mark, then take admission to learn the holiest book of Almighty.

Who is this course for?

Kids

Perfect for children starting from age 4-5.

Adults

Beginners who want to fix their pronunciation.

Reverts

Guidance for new Muslims starting from zero.

What You Will Learn?

  • Proper Makharij (Origins) of every Arabic letter.
  • Recognition of Arabic Alphabets and joining forms.
  • Definitions of Hadar and Tarteel (Reading speed).
  • Rules of Harakaat, Tanween, and Maddah.
  • Basic rules of Tajweed applicable to Nazra.
  • Obligatory teachings: Salah, Wudu, and Kalimas.

Our Teaching Roadmap

Phase 01: Identification

Focusing on single letters (Mufradat) and their correct sounds.

Phase 02: Word Formation

Learning how letters join together (Murakkabat) to form words.

Phase 03: Quran Recitation

Starting with Para 30 and applying all Tajweed rules live.
